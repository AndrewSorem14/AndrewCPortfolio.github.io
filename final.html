Final Content

Description:

My final project in ECS was on scratch. This was a series of games/questions where you have to do all of them consistently to win.

In the first game you have to fly around in the airplane collecting the orange coins. Whenever you collect one, another one appears in a different locaition on the screen. Collect 10 coins in 30 seconds to win

In the second game you have to click the correct button with the right answer so be prepared.

In the last game you have to fight a boss called "Rhyperior" using "Vaporeon." Since Vaporeon is super affective against Rhyperior this is a pretty easy boss fight, but it's an epic battle

If you can do all of these three things consistently you will win the game.
